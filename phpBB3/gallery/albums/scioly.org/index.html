Science Olympiad Student Center

Founded in 1998, the Science Olympiad Student Center has grown to become one of the most important resources to Science Olympiad participants endeavoring to improve their scientific knowledge. Over the past two decades, the site has grown to include features such as an image gallery, housing thousands of images relating to Science Olympiad events; a wiki, with hundreds of pages of knowledge provided by Science Olympiad participants and alumni; a test exchange, filled with practice tests for nearly every event; and an active message board and chat system with plenty of fellow students and graduates willing to help answer any question.
